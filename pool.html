Infinity Pool

Enjoy the breeze and a magnificent scenery as you take a dip into our infinity pool.

If blue is your favorite color, this is the place for you. The infinity pool is constructed to look like it is connected to the ever-bluer waters of the sea and skies. You will be delighted as you relax and have fun with your friend not only at day time, but also until the skies are filled with lustrous stars at night.



Kiddie Pool

Let the kids have fun and enjoy the cool waters of our kiddie pool. Located just below the infinity pool, it provides a waterfall kind of effect that the kids would truly enjoy.



JoeCo's Bar & Cafe


If you need a quick fix of your cravings or thirst, JoeCo's Bar & Cafe is the place to fill you up. Located just beside the infinity pool, you can get a wide variety of food and drink selections.

From nachos, cakes, and sandwitches to cocktails, wine, and beers, be prepared to tickle your taste buds and beat the summer heat.
