Dalaroy Swimming Pool Officially Open

Dalaroy Beach Resort is proud to announce that the Swimming Pool is officially open to the public!

March 30, 2019 marks the opening of the Infinity Swimming Pool of Dalaroy Beach Resort to the public which engraves a major milestone for the Resorts. The design of the Swiming pool area has a modern approach of being simple and classy but at the same time fun for relaxation and Photo opportunities, as it provides a breath-taking view of the beach and a connection to the ever-bluer waters of the sea and skies. Not only that, children can also have a good time in our Kiddie Pool. For only Php 250.00, everyone can enjoy the infinity pool during day time and Php 350.00 for 24 hours.

Welcome to Dalaroy Beach Resort!

On the shores of Cavite, there stand the quaint nipa huts and cottages of Dalaroy Beach Resort. Stunning views of the horizon during sunset on the resort’s beach and cool, fresh breeze make it the perfect romantic getaway. Its low rates are easy on the wallet for the traveller on a budget, and make it the ideal place for students on a vacation with friends. The friendly and attentive staff is always very welcoming and are there to assist you with your needs.
